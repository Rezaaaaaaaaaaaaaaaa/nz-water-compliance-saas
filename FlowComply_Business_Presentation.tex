\section{Executive Overview}

FlowComply is an AI-powered water intelligence platform that transforms water quality data into actionable insights. Originally developed for regulatory compliance, the platform's advanced capabilities make it ideal for research applications including wastewater surveillance, antimicrobial resistance monitoring, and contaminant fate studies.

\subsection{Core Value Proposition}

\begin{highlightbox}
\textbf{80\% reduction in manual analysis} through automated monitoring, AI-powered anomaly detection, and intelligent reportingâ€”while improving detection accuracy and response times.
\end{highlightbox}

\subsection{Key Capabilities}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, itemsep=0.2em]
    \item \textbf{AI-Powered Analysis:} Claude 3.5 Sonnet integration for intelligent interpretation
    \item \textbf{Real-Time Monitoring:} Multi-parameter tracking with automated alerts
    \item \textbf{Anomaly Detection:} Statistical and ML-based quality excursion identification
    \item \textbf{Predictive Analytics:} Trend analysis and early warning systems
    \item \textbf{Research-Ready:} Unlimited historical storage, comprehensive API access

\section{AI-Powered Intelligence}

\subsection{1. Water Quality Anomaly Detection}

Automatically analyzes test results to identify:
\begin{itemize}[itemsep=0.2em]
    \item Statistical anomalies beyond historical norms
    \item Trend changes (gradual degradation/improvement)
    \item Multi-parameter correlations
    \item Seasonal patterns and treatment efficacy
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Monitored Parameters:} E. coli, protozoa, pH, turbidity, chlorine, nitrates, heavy metals, organic compounds

\subsection{2. Intelligent Compliance Assistant}

24/7 conversational AI providing instant answers on:
\begin{itemize}[itemsep=0.2em]
    \item Regulatory requirements and standards
    \item Treatment optimization recommendations
    \item Incident response procedures
    \item Best practice guidance
\end{itemize}

\subsection{3. Automated Report Generation}

AI-generated summaries including:
\begin{itemize}[itemsep=0.2em]
    \item Executive compliance performance overviews
    \item Trend analysis over custom periods
    \item Predictive insights for future performance
    \item Regulatory-ready exports (PDF, Excel, CSV)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{4. Document Analysis}

Analyzes water safety plans to:
\begin{itemize}[itemsep=0.2em]
    \item Identify missing mandatory elements
    \item Assess regulatory completeness (0-100 score)
    \item Provide severity-ranked recommendations
    \item Generate gap analysis reports
\end{itemize}
